About VibeFlow

We are redefining how candidates apply for jobs. VibeFlow is a next-generation AI-powered resume analysis platform designed to bridge the gap between talented job seekers and rigid Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Our Mission

Every year, millions of highly qualified candidates are rejected by automated ATS bots simply because of formatting errors, missing keywords, or poor phrasing. We believe that talent should not be blocked by bad algorithms.

Our mission is to democratize the job search process. We built VibeFlow to give you the exact same AI tools that companies use to filter you out—so you can beat them at their own game.

Empowering 10,000+ Careers

By providing military-grade ATS insights to the public entirely for free.

Core Capabilities

🤖

AI Neural Engine

Powered by local LLMs (Phi-3 & TinyLlama) for deep contextual and semantic understanding of your experience.

🔍

ATS Simulation

Simulates real-world enterprise recruiter filters (like Workday, Taleo) to test your resume structure.

📊

Dynamic Analytics

Advanced visual tracking with Radar charts, Doughnut matching, and historical upload trends.

💡

Smart Suggestions

Get actionable, line-by-line AI advice on how to rewrite weak bullet points into high-impact metrics.

Development Roadmap

Phase 1: Proof of Concept

Built basic Python PDF extraction scripts and tested regex algorithms against simple textual rules.

Phase 2: Deep AI Integration

Integrated Ollama to allow offline, private grading using Phi-3 natively. Setup complex structured prompt engineering for reliable outputs.

Phase 3: The UI/UX Upgrade (Current)

Revamped the frontend entirely to feature interactive Glassmorphism, 3D Tilt Cards, historical tracking, and robust AI rewriting chatbots.

Phase 4: Global Scale

Future plans include cloud API deployment, live Google Docs extension, and enterprise recruiter portals.
